"The token \"over\" is used to indicate a range or extent, often in a physical or temporal sense, and is frequently accompanied by a noun or adjective describing the extent, such as \"over time\", \"over a long period\", \"over the past year\", \"over two and a half\", \"over 3500\", \"over 2 million\", \"over 78,895\", \"over sea level\", \"over 13,000 words\", \"over 1,350 basic roots\", \"over 250 surviving Tuskegee Airmen\", and \"over 2,000 pounds\"."
