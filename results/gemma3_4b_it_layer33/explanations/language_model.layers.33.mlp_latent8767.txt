"The token \"un\" is often used as a prefix to convey a sense of negation, absence, or lack, describing something as not being, or not being in a certain state, such as \"unhappy\", \"unreal\", \"unpardonable\", \"unright\", \"unregeneracy\", \"unholy\", \"unvarnished\", \"unromantic\", \"unimpactful\", \"unluckily\", \"unrecognisable\", \"ungodly\", and \"unloved\"."
