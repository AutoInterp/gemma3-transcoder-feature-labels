"The token \"all\" is often used as a determiner in phrases indicating a complete set or group, such as \"all downstream Total Maximum Daily Load (TMDL) impaired waters\", \"all libraries will be open\", \"all commerce in the city ceased\", and \"all necessary data fields are present\". It is also used in phrases indicating universality, such as \"all over the world\", \"all those creating $100,000\", and \"all my test of verification\"."
