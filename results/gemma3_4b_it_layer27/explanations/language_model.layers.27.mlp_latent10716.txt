"The token \"off\" is used in various idiomatic expressions, including phrases indicating deviation from a standard or norm, such as \"off-the-shelf\", \"offbeat\", \"off-base\", and \"off-road\". It is also used to indicate a departure from a usual or expected location, such as \"off-campus\", \"off-center\", and \"off the beaten path\".
